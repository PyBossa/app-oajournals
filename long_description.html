Classify some Journals!

Jevin West has kindly made available the list of 985 journals and their publishers that he uses in his excellent OA plot here.

And we would like to build on this data to assess the licence used by each one of those 985 journals to publish their articles.

We have started the task on a collaborative PyBossa app, where all you have to do is to Google the journal name, and select from a set of options the license for the Journal and who owns the Copyright.

All data filled-in on the datasheet will be public domain data for anyone to use/copy/remix CC0
